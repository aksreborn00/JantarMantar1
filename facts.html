Fun Facts About Jantar Mantar

🕰️ It tells time to within 2 seconds of accuracy using only shadows.
🌍 There are 5 Jantar Mantars across India—Delhi, Jaipur, Ujjain, Mathura & Varanasi.
📐 The Samrat Yantra is 27 meters tall and works like a massive sundial.
🔭 Jantar Mantar means “calculation instrument” in Sanskrit.
🚫 Not a single lens or telescope is used—only geometry and sunlight.
